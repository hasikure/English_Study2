Writing + Speaking

Topic

When you need a break after studying or working, do you prefer going outside for a short walk, or staying indoors and relaxing?

Think about situations like after a long class, during a lunch break, on a rainy day, after work, or during exam week.

Task 1: Speaking

Speak in English first for 45 to 60 seconds.

Include:

  • which option you usually prefer
  • two reasons
  • one real example from your life
  • one situation where your choice might change

Task 2: Japanese Draft

Write what you really wanted to say in Japanese.

You can include:

  • ふだんはどちらを選ぶか
  • その理由
  • 最近の具体例
  • ただし例外もあること

Task 3: English Writing

Write it in English in 4 to 6 sentences.

Try to connect your ideas with words like:

  • usually
  • because
  • for example
  • however

Task 4: Ideal Version

After writing your own English, compare it with this sample.

I usually prefer going outside for a short walk when I need a break after studying or working. Moving my body helps me refresh my mind, and even ten minutes of fresh air can change my mood. For example, when I feel tired after a long afternoon of work, I often walk around my neighborhood before dinner. However, if the weather is bad or I am very exhausted, I would rather stay indoors and relax with music or tea.

Task 5: Gap Check

  • Expressions you could not produce: for example, "refresh my mind," "get some fresh air," "feel exhausted"
  • Expressions that sounded unnatural: check articles, verb tense, and whether your comparison sounded smooth
  • Expressions you want to reuse next time: "even ten minutes of...," "change my mood," "I would rather..."

Task 6: Re-Speaking

Speak again after revision for 45 to 60 seconds.

This time, try to:

  • use at least two expressions from Task 5
  • speak more smoothly without reading word by word
  • add one extra detail or example
