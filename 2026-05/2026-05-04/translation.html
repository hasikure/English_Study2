Ideal Version

By the time I got to the station this morning, the train was already more than twenty minutes late because of an accident. While I was waiting on the platform, I messaged my boss that I might be late and reviewed the meeting materials on my phone. In the end, I made it to the office before the meeting started, but next time I think I'll look into another route as well.

Focus

  • By the time ... , ... already ... is a useful pattern for 「〜したときには、すでに…だった」.
  • might be late sounds natural when you want to say 「遅れるかもしれない」 without sounding too certain.
  • look into another route is a natural way to say 「別の行き方も調べる」.
